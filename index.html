about

Hey! I’m Alexandre, a multimedia and visual art enthusiast, former student of the multimedia integration technique program at Cégep Montpetit and currently a student at UQAT's Montreal campus in its video game integration concentration.

I know my way around making websites, using languages suchs as HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript, and I also design interfaces for websites, applications, and video games alike, using tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma.

I am both a perfectionist and hardworking person, always determined to better myself and learn new things with each new project.
